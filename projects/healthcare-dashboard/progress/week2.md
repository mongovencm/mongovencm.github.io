# Week 2

- What did you do last week?
  
This past week I finalized my project proposal and further refined the scope of the project. I continued to review the SoccerMon dataset and confirmed that I will stick to my initial idea and focus primarily on the subjective athlete monitoring data. The largest categories are related to training load and athlete wellness, and there are smaller sets including injury, illness and performance. I hope to incorporate each of the categories in some way for my dashboard. 

I also continued familiarizing myself with Streamlit and reviewed examples of dashboards and data visualization layouts to better understand how I want to organize the application. 

- What do you plan to do this week?

This week, since I didn't get to as much of it last week, I plan to make improvements to my website. I also plan to begin working directly with the dataset. My goals are to download and inspect the data files to start coming up with some measures and metrics I want to display. 

- Are there any impediments in your way?
  
There are currently no major impediments in my way. A challenge will likely be understanding the structure of the dataset and deciding which metrics provide the most useful information for the dashboard while keeping the project focused and manageable.

- Reflection on the process you used last week. How can you make the process work better?
  
I think the time I spent refining the project scope and reviewing the dataset this past week was valuable. It helped me feel more confident about the overall direction of the project and the technologies I will be using. At the same time, I feel like I could have pushed myself forward and begun some initial data importing and more environment setup. One area I would like to improve is transitioning from planning into implementation. Now that the project scope is more clearly defined, I can begin creating tangible pieces of the application rather than focusing primarily on research and planning.
